Features

Here you will find the games that MPS Studios have created.

MPS 4-a-side

MPS 4-a-side is the newest and most popular game of MPS Studios with unexpected surprises.

The objective of the game is for the teams to score seven goals before the opposing team does. Unlike traditional football/soccer, there are no out of bounds or offsides in MPS 4-a-side.

The game maintains the roots of MPS while also incorporating a distinct style. If you are interested in playing or learning more about the game, you can visit the official game page on Roblox for the latest news.

MPS 4-a-side Legacy

This was the first game that MPS studios released. Which consists of different features such as, different react, smaller map, and ball.

MPS 4-a-side Legacy is an older version of the MPS 4-a-side game.

This game follows a similar concept to MPS 4-a-side, which is a simple but fast-paced football/soccer game.

The objective of the game is for the first team to score seven goals to win, and there are no out of bounds or offsides. The game maintains the roots of MPS while also incorporating a unique style.

English Premier League

Is a competitive Roblox football league. It is designed to provide a unique and competitive experience with features that set it apart from other leagues.

If you are interested in getting involved in the Roblox English Premier League, it is recommended to join their official Discord server.

This can provide you with an opportunity to connect with other players, stay updated on league activities, and potentially participate in matches or competitions.
